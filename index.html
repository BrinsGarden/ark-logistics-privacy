Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Ark Logistics ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Ark Logistics mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App. This App is intended for internal use by Ark Logistics employees only.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username and password used to log into the App
  • Employee Profile Data: Full name, username, and role (manager or employee)
  • Operational Data: Shipment records, task assignments, issue reports, dock check records, and time-off requests
  • Photos: Images uploaded to document shipments, tasks, issues, and dock checks
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens used to send operational alerts

How We Use Your Information

All information is used solely for internal warehouse operations including tracking shipments, managing tasks, reporting issues, conducting dock checks, managing time-off requests, and generating PDF records. We do not use your information for advertising or any purpose outside Ark Logistics internal operations.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to authenticated Ark Logistics employees only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or as necessary to operate the App via Google Firebase. All data remains within Ark Logistics internal systems.

Photos and Camera Access

The App requests access to your device's photo library to allow employees to attach photos to records. Photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage and are only accessible to Ark Logistics employees. We do not access your camera or photo library for any purpose other than what you explicitly initiate within the App.

Push Notifications

The App may send push notifications for task assignments, approvals, and operational alerts. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings without affecting App functionality.

Data Retention

Operational records are retained for business record-keeping purposes. Employee account data is removed upon termination of employment. You may request deletion of your data by contacting your manager or the App administrator.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adults in a professional workplace setting. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
