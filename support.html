About This App

The Ark Logistics app is an internal operations tool for Ark Logistics warehouse employees. It is used to track shipments, manage tasks, report issues, conduct dock checks, and manage time-off requests.

This app is not available to the general public. Access is provided exclusively to Ark Logistics employees.

Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my username or password. What do I do?
Contact your manager or supervisor. Only managers can reset employee passwords through the User Management section of the app.
The app won't let me log in.
Make sure you're entering your username (not your email) and the correct password. If the problem persists, contact your manager to verify your account is active in the system.
I'm not receiving push notifications.
Go to your phone's Settings → find the Ark Logistics app → make sure Notifications are enabled. On iPhone, also check that notifications are allowed in TestFlight settings.
My photos aren't uploading.
Make sure the app has permission to access your photo library. Go to Settings → Ark Logistics → Photos and select "All Photos." Also check that you have a stable internet connection.
The app is showing old data or not updating.
Close the app completely and reopen it. The app syncs data in real time when connected to the internet. Make sure you have a working Wi-Fi or cellular connection.
How do I report a bug or request a new feature?
Contact your manager or email us directly at the address below. Please describe the issue in as much detail as possible including what screen you were on and what happened.
I'm an iPhone user — how do I install the app?
You need to install TestFlight from the App Store first (it's free). Then tap the invite link sent to your email by Apple. The Ark Logistics app will appear inside TestFlight and you can install it from there.
I'm an Android user — how do I install the app?
Your manager will send you the APK file via email or messaging. Tap the file on your Android phone and follow the prompts to install. You may need to allow installation from unknown sources in your settings.
